## Submission Guardrails

- Do not invent clinical facts, signatures, failed therapies, contraindications, or payer criteria.
- Do not include uncensored PHI, full member IDs, SSNs, credentials, payment card data, or secrets in public prompts.
- Do not submit an appeal, external review, grievance, fax, email, or portal action without explicit owner authorization.
