## Issue Evidence

- Misconduct or discharge: policy, final incident, warnings, investigation, direct witness.
- Voluntary quit: resignation reason, dated timeline, preservation attempts, supporting records.
- Able/available/work search: certifications, work-search log, restrictions, job-offer or refusal records.
- Overpayment: overpayment notice, affected weeks, payment history, appeal or waiver deadline.

## Guardrails

- Redact SSNs, claimant IDs, portal credentials, bank data, full medical details, and unrelated personal facts.
- Do not file, upload, withdraw, request subpoenas, or contact the agency without explicit authorized owner action.
- Verify jurisdiction-specific deadlines and procedures with the agency, representative, legal aid, counsel, or official notice.
