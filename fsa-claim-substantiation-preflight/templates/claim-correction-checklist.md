# FSA Claim Correction Checklist

Use this as a local packet checklist before resubmission. Do not include full SSNs, full card numbers, credentials, or unrelated medical details.

- Review owner:

## Required Evidence

- [ ] Itemized receipt or provider statement.
- [ ] Patient/dependent name visible.
- [ ] Provider/vendor/caregiver name visible.
- [ ] Date of service or care visible.
- [ ] Service/item description visible.
- [ ] Amount charged or patient responsibility visible.
- [ ] EOB included when insurance processed the service.
- [ ] LMN or prescription included when required by plan category.
- [ ] Dependent-care provider certification/signature/TIN included when required.
- [ ] Coverage period, runout deadline, or deductible-met proof included when relevant.
- [ ] Duplicate reimbursement check completed.

## Resubmission Notes

- Highlight page and field locations instead of uploading extra unrelated documents.
- Keep a dated call log or portal message log.
- Ask the administrator to identify the exact missing substantiation element when denial language is vague.
- Keep final submission, appeal, repayment, payroll, and tax decisions with the authorized owner.
