# Adjuster Packet Checklist

## Claim Context

- Policyholder name redacted for public prompts.
- Claim number redacted unless submitting directly to the insurer.
- Loss date and loss type.
- Insurer-provided contents form, if available.
- Relevant policy declarations, personal-property limit, deductible, and sublimit pages.

## Inventory Files

- Contents inventory spreadsheet.
- Room-by-room photo or video index.
- Receipts, purchase-history exports, appraisals, warranties, manuals, or serial/model records.
- Replacement-cost support such as quotes, catalog pages, or current equivalent product links.
- Damage/loss evidence such as damaged-item photos, room-loss photos, theft report references, restoration inventories, or total-loss notes.

## Review Before Submission

- High-value rows have ownership evidence and replacement-cost support.
- Age and condition are filled for material rows.
- Jewelry, firearms, collectibles, art, cash, business property, tools, and specialty categories are separated for policy review.
- Duplicate-looking rows and large quantities are explained or grouped.
- Recovered, repairable, or salvageable items have consistent notes.
- Communication log tracks every adjuster request, submitted file, and response date.

## Boundaries

- Do not fabricate evidence.
- Do not include SSNs, payment data, credentials, or unrelated family records.
- Do not treat this checklist as legal, coverage, valuation, tax, construction, or public-adjuster advice.
