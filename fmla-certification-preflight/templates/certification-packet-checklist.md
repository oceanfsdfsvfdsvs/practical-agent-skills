## Owner Questions

- What exact fields did HR/leave administrator say are missing or insufficient?
- Was the cure window documented in writing?
- Is an extension request or good-faith-effort record needed?
- Who is authorized to contact HR, the provider, the leave administrator, union, counsel, or agency?

## Guardrails

- Redact SSNs, claim numbers, portal credentials, full medical records, employee IDs, and unrelated personnel data.
- Do not submit, deny, approve, upload, or contact anyone without authorized owner action.
